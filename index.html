Meet the Racers

Victor Stasek

Racing again as the lovely Girl Villager, Victor is the current undefeated champion. Victor has perfected his Mario Kart skills with hours of practicing. Will it be enough to keep the trophy and remain the undefeated champion?

Jacob Althouse

Jacob returns to the tournament this year racing again as Pink Gold Peach. King of green shells, you better be fast to keep up with him!

Shannon Stasek

Shannon is racing this year as someone new, Male Villager! She has been practicing hard - learning how to drift and properly use items. Does she have what it takes?

Scott Haddox

Hyaa! Racing as Hyrule’s hero, Link, Scottie zooms down the track. You couldn’t pay him enough rupees to slow down!

Andrew Ferguson

New to the tournament this year, Andrew is racing as Black Yoshi. Little is known about Andrew’s racing style. Will we see an upset in this year’s tournament?

Isaiah Holt

Keeping that pussy platinum, Isaiah storms the track racing as Rosalina. Coming in a close second last year, will Isaiah have what it takes to take home the trophy this year?

Israel Holt

Racing as the hardcore Toadette, Izzy dominates the track while looking cute as can be. He takes no shit from anyone and gives no fucks with his item usage.

Aidan Gipe

Aidan keeps it real racing as the Lil Uzi Vert look-a-like, Black Inkling. Watch out for his sick flow!

Madisson Banks

Also racing as Rosalina, Madisson speeds through the tracks on her cycle. She dominates those curves and turns all while looking bad ass.

Cheyenne Ferguson

Also new to the tournament this year, Cheyenne is racing as Green Girl Inkling. Combined, Cheyenne and Andrew have put over 60 hours into Mario Kart. They’re eager for the trophy!

Matt Collmar

An unknown racer and unknown racing style... Matt comes into this race leaving all other racers blind. Having never raced before, it will be fun to see what he can bring to the competition!
